Tested on Google Chrome. On Firefox there can be problems with some sound effects.



Controls:
.)Movement with arrow keys
.)Space for jumping (it's possible to jump over enemies, but not over walls)



All foundations and basic aspects are implemented for Lab3.

Also implemented:
.)Two enemy ghosts
.)Continuous movement
.)Background music and sound effects
.)Point counter

Shadows are not implemented however.
